All About Morgan Wallen

Biography

Morgan Wallen is an American country music singer and songwriter. He was born on May 13, 1993, in Sneedville, Tennessee, making him 32 years old in 2025. Growing up in a small town, Wallen was introduced to music early in life and first pursued dreams of becoming a professional baseball player before shifting his focus fully to music.

Wallen rose to fame after competing on the television show The Voice in 2014. Since then, he has become one of the most popular artists in modern country music, known for blending traditional country sounds with elements of rock and hip hop. His albums, such as Dangerous: The Double Album and One Thing at a Time, have broken records and gained massive popularity worldwide.
